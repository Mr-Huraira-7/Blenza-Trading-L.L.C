About Us

A business built around dependable trade relationships, digital services, and clean execution.

Blenza Trading L.L.C serves businesses that need a reliable partner for sourcing coordination, wholesale movement, software development, website development, and structured business communication across markets.

Company Overview

A corporate business with a practical, service-led approach across trade and technology.
